    Who Is David Fuller?

    David Fuller Wikipedia: - David Fuller works as an electrician in English hospitals and has a criminal record for murders. He was born on September 4, 1954, in Deal, Kent, England. He was found guilty in 2021 of the 1987 Bedsit murders in Royal Tunbridge Wells, Kent, of strangling and sexually abusing 20-year-old Caroline Pierce and 25-year-old Wendy Knell.

    The killings occurred months apart. When Fuller was eventually caught, he was sentenced to 12 years in prison for mortuary offenses. During his time working as an electrician at the Kent and Sussex Hospital in Tunbridge Wells and the Tunbridge Wells Hospital in nearby Pembury, he had admitted to mistreating the bodies of over 100 female corpses.

    On December 15, 2021, he was sentenced to life in prison with a whole life order, which means he will carry out his term without being eligible for parole. Fuller entered a guilty plea on November 3, 2022, to allegations that he had committed an additional 16 offenses between 2007 and 2020 at mortuaries in the now-closed Kent and Sussex Hospital and its replacement, the Tunbridge Wells Hospital at Pembury, which were filed in October 2021.
