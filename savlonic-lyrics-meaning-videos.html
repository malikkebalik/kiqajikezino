    Overall Meaning

    The lyrics of Savlonic's Epoch speak about the theme of mistakes, remorse, and redemption. The song highlights that everyone makes mistakes and the realization of the fault is the first step towards correcting them. The singer admits that they have made more mistakes than their share, but they are determined to repair it. The lyrics imply that people shouldn't be afraid of making mistakes because the process of learning and evolving comes after acknowledging them. The line "But I just don't agree, no, this show Is of my own making," indicates that people have the power to take control of their lives and make things better.


    The theme of the song exemplifies that humans can always move forward from their past wrongdoings and improve. The lyrics also highlight the importance of owning up to one's mistakes and learning from them. The line "Our fates are not yet written" emphasizes that people have the power to change the course of their lives by making the right choices. In conclusion, Epoch is a song that encourages the listeners to learn from their mistakes, redeem themselves, and make their lives better.
