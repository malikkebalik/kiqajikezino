    Bismarck Police searching for missing 81: An 81-year-old man has gone missing in Bismarck, North Dakota, and the local police are seeking the public’s help in locating him. Lloyd Liken was last seen leaving his residence on Turnbow Lane in a gold Toyota Highlander on November 16th. Despite having gotten lost twice before, Liken does not meet the criteria for a Silver Alert. With no phone and unknown medical condition, authorities are urging anyone with information to contact the Bismarck Police Department.

    Missing 81-Year-Old Man in Bismarck

    Authorities in Bismarck are seeking the public’s assistance in locating an 81-year-old man who has gone missing. The Bismarck Police Department is urging anyone with information to come forward and help in the search for Lloyd Liken.

    Request for Information

    The Bismarck Police Department is appealing to the community for any information that could aid in finding Mr. Liken. If you have seen or heard anything that might be relevant to his disappearance, please reach out to the authorities immediately.

    Last Known Location and Vehicle

    According to the police, Mr. Liken was last seen leaving the 4400 block of Turnbow Lane in his gold Toyota Highlander on Thursday, November 16, at approximately 8 p.m. This information could be crucial in determining his current whereabouts.

    Unknown Medical Condition and Attire

    Unfortunately, the police do not have any details regarding Mr. Liken’s medical condition or the clothing he was wearing when he went missing. Any information about his health or attire could greatly assist in the search efforts.

    Description of the Missing Person

    Lloyd Liken is described as a 6-foot-tall individual weighing around 200 pounds. He has grey hair and captivating blue eyes. These distinctive features can help people identify him if they have seen him recently.

    Past Incidents of Getting Lost

    It is worth noting that Mr. Liken has previously gotten lost on two occasions and was located by law enforcement. This information highlights the importance of finding him as soon as possible to ensure his safety and well-being.

    Not Meeting Criteria for Silver Alert

    Although Mr. Liken does not meet the criteria for a Silver Alert, the urgency to locate him remains high. The police are treating this case with utmost seriousness and are actively pursuing any leads that may arise.

    Contact Information for Providing Information

    If you have any information regarding the whereabouts of Lloyd Liken, please contact the Bismarck Police Department at 701-223-1212. Your assistance could be vital in reuniting Mr. Liken with his loved ones.
