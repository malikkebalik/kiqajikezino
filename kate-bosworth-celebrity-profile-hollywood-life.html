    Bio:
    Kate Bosworth (born Catherine Ann Bosworth on January 2, 1983 in Los Angeles, California) is an American actress and model. She made her film debut in The Horse Whisperer in 1998. She went on the get a supporting role in Remember the Titans. Her breakthrough role came in 2002 as the lead in surfing chick-flick Blue Crush. In 2004, Kate starred in the rom-com, Win a Date with Tad Hamilton!, but the film ended up being a flop. Her other films included Beyond the Sea, for which she received critical acclaim, and Bee Season. In 2006, Kate portrayed Lois Lane in Superman Returns and went on to receive roles in Bringing Down The House, 21, and The Warriors Way. In the 2010s, Kate moved into fashion and designed a jewelry line with Cher Coulter called JewelMint. Their expanded into the websites, JewelMint and BeachMint. Most recently, Kate starred alongside Julianne Moore in Still Alice.

    Best Known For:
    Kate Bosworth is best known for her leading role in Blue Crush.

    Personal Life:
    Kate was accepted to attend Princeton University, but deferred her acceptance to focus on her acting career. Kate dated actor Orlando Bloom from 2003 to September 2006.. In July 2011, Kate and her boyfriend, actor Alexander Skarsgard broke up after two years of dating. In mid-2011, she began dating director Michael Polish, who she met while filming the movie Big Sur. They were engaged in August 2012 and married August 2013.
