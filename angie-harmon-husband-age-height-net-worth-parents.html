    Angie Harmon Wikipedia: - Angela Harmon is an American actress and model who was born on 10 August 1972 in Highland Park, Texas, U.S. She started her career in 1987 working as a child model, and achieved a Seventeen modeling contest.  She has a contract with New York City's IMG Models.

    In 1995, Harmon started her acting career after David Hasselhoff noticed her on an aircraft. She also starred in the 1998 movie Lawn Dogs, which was released in a small number of theaters. She played Boston police investigator Jane Rizzoli in the crime drama series Rizzoli & Isles on TNT from 2010 to 2016, costarring with Sasha Alexander. Harmon had an appearance in the January 2012 music video for "This Ole Boy" by Craig Morgan. She inked a multi-picture exclusive agreement with Lifetime in 2021.
