    Who is A.J. Calloway?

    A. J. Calloway is an American Entertainment Reporter best known as the original host on 106 & Park, which aired on BET as well as the Co-Host of the entertainment news program, ‘Extra’.

    How old is AJ Calloway?

    A. J. Calloway was born on the 29th of August, 1974 in New Jersey, the U.S. As of 2023, his age is 48. Although he hasn’t made the names of his parents public, we do know for a fact that he spent his childhood days in New Jersey.

    His ethnicity is African-American.

    Education: School/College, University

    Talking about his education, he attended St. Benedict’s Prep School. He later attended Howard University, Washington D.C. for further education.

    A. J. Calloway: Professional Life, Career

    After graduating from Howard University, he began his professional career in New York as a journalist. The role that made him come out in the limelight came in the form of the Original Host of the show 106 & Park, which aired on BET (Black Entertainment Television).

    He co-hosted the show alongside Marie Antoinette Wright (Free, on the show) for nearly five years before leaving the show on July 28, 2005.

    After 106 & Park, Calloway went on to Co-host the entertainment news program Extra for a long fourteen years from 2005 to 2019.

    He was a good reporter on the show and also acted as a representation of the African-Americans in entertainment TV. Calloway went on to win two Emmy Awards for Outstanding Entertainment News program in 2014 and 2015.

    He was recently fired by Warner Brothers from Extra after the studio investigated multiple allegations of sexual charges filed against him.

    A. J. Calloway: Net Worth, Salary

    As of 2022, A. J. Calloway has an estimated net worth of around $4 million. His current job is unknown as he was recently fired and he had not made information on his salary public.

    However, we do know that an average American entertainment reporter with a few years of experience makes around 52k U.S. dollars annually.

    We can assume his salary to have been higher because he was a famous TV personality as Extra was a popular show.

    Sexual Assaults and Rape Allegations

    A. J. Calloway was fired from Extra back in 2019 after Warner Brothers investigated the multiple claims of sexual assaults and even rape allegations made against Calloway.

    The earliest account dates back to 2003 and the most recent was in 2013. Warner Bros was criticized for taking too much time before taking action against Calloway. As of now, he has been accused by 6 women of sexual assault and misconduct and among them, three involved alleged rape.

    Calloway however, has vehemently denied all of the allegations against him, saying that he has never assaulted anyone and looks forward to clearing his name.
