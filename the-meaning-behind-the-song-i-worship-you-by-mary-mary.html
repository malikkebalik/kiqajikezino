    The Meaning Behind The Song: I Worship You by Mary Mary

    I am listening to the song I Worship You by Mary Mary as I write this article. It is a powerful worship song that always manages to touch my heart and soul. The lyrics of this song carry a deep meaning that resonates with many people, including myself.

    23 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Introduction

    I am listening to the song “I Worship You” by Mary Mary as I write this article. It is a powerful worship song that always manages to touch my heart and soul. The lyrics of this song carry a deep meaning that resonates with many people, including myself.

    The Journey from Lost to Found

    The song begins with the lines, “I was lost but You came and found me, You left Your throne thinking about me.” These words remind me of the unconditional love and grace that God extends to all of us. It speaks of God’s willingness to leave his comfort and sacrifice everything just to find and save us. This is a beautiful reminder of the lengths God went to show His mercy and love to humanity.

    Filling the Empty and Bringing Sight

    The next verse says, “I was empty but You came and filled me, I was blinded but You helped me to see.” These lines express the transformative power of God’s love in our lives. It talks about how God’s presence can fill the void within us and provide us with a sense of purpose and completeness. Moreover, it highlights how God can bring clarity and understanding to our lives, even when we are blinded by our own limitations.

    From Brokenness to Wholeness

    Another verse says, “I was broken but You made me whole again, I felt like nothing but You gave me confidence.” This part of the song speaks to me on a personal level. It reminds me of the times when I felt broken and hopeless, but God’s love and grace lifted me up and restored my faith in myself. It serves as a reminder that through God, we can find healing and restoration, even in our darkest moments.

    A Heart Overflowing with Gratitude

    The chorus of the song emphasizes the overwhelming gratitude that the singer feels towards God. The lyrics say, “And I’m filled so much with gratitude words are not enough, to explain the magnitude, the passion of my love.” These words remind me to pause and reflect on the immeasurable love and blessings that God has poured into my life. It encourages me to have a heart filled with gratitude for all that God has done and continues to do for me.

    A Declaration of Worship

    As the song progresses, it repeats the lines, “You took everything I was, made me what I am, and with all I am I worship You.” This powerful declaration of worship reminds me that worship is not just a musical expression but a lifestyle, a way of acknowledging and honoring God in all aspects of our lives. It encourages me to make every moment of my daily existence an act of worship to God.

    Closing Thoughts

    “I Worship You” by Mary Mary is a beautiful and heartfelt song that captures the essence of our relationship with God. It speaks of His love, transformation, and the overwhelming gratitude we experience in response to His blessings. This song has personally touched my spirit and reminds me of the depth of God’s love and His constant presence in my life.

    Listening to this song while working, playing games, or watching a football match brings a sense of comfort and reassurance. It reminds me that no matter what I am doing, I can always turn my attention towards God and offer Him my worship.

    So, I encourage you to take a few moments to listen to “I Worship You” by Mary Mary. Allow the lyrics to penetrate your heart and remind you of the profound love and mercy that God extends to you. Let it inspire you to live a life of worship and gratitude, acknowledging God’s presence in every aspect of your life.
