    Brad Pitt's awards and acheivements

    Brad Pitt's social media accounts

    In an era where people and celebrities alike are immersed in the frenzy of social media, Brad Pitt stands out as one of the few who has consistently chosen to steer clear of it.

    The actor's aversion to social media was addressed by a reporter in 2019, questioning when he might create an Instagram account. Pitt's response was a firm, "Never gonna happen."

    Pitt went on to elaborate on his decision, stating, "Well, I never say never. Life’s pretty good without it. I don’t see the point." 

    Notably, Pitt acknowledged that social media could have been a tool to dispel misconceptions in his earlier days.

    Reflecting on this, he remarked, “And if I’d have that in my younger days, I’d have used it. Because I felt quite used, and completely misunderstood, and misread, and not given the benefit of the doubt,” as per firstcuriousity.
