    Jerry Trainor- Biography

    Jerry Trainor (Gerald William Trainor) is an actor, comedian, and musician from the United States. He is 44 years old and was born on January 21, 1977. He is most known for his roles as Crazy Steve, Dudley Puppy, and Spencer Shay on the Nickelodeon sitcoms Drake & Josh, T.U.F.F. Puppy, and iCarly.

    He earned three Kids’ Choice Awards for his portrayal of Spencer Shay. Jerry Trainor was nominated for a Daytime Emmy Award as well. He has also appeared in programs such as 2 Broke Girls and Crossing Jordan.

    Jerry Trainor was born in San Diego, California, and worked at SeaWorld before becoming an actor.
