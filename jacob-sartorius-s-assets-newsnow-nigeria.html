    Jacob Sartorius Wiki Biography

    Born on October 2, 2002, in Oklahoma, USA, Rolf Jacob Sartorius is an internet sensation recognized for his Vine videos. He is also a singer, known for the song “Sweatshirt” and EP “The Last Text”. His journey in the entertainment industry began in 2014.

    As of late 2017, Jacob Sartorius’s net worth is estimated to be over $500,000, earned through his successful career as an internet personality and singer.

    Jacob Sartorius Net Worth $500,000

    Jacob Sartorius was raised by adoptive parents in Reston, Virginia, as his biological parents were unable to support him financially. He attended various high schools before being homeschooled.

    At the age of 12, Jacob started his journey in the entertainment industry by creating a profile on Vine, where he gained popularity by posting videos of himself singing and lip-syncing popular songs.

    In 2016, he signed a recording contract with an independent record company and released his hit single “Sweatshirt”, which peaked at No. 90 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart, significantly contributing to his net worth. He also released an official music video for the song on his YouTube channel, which has garnered almost 50 million views to date.

    Later, Jacob joined the MagCon Tour and released his first EP, “The Last Text,” in 2017, which helped in further increasing his popularity and net worth.

    Jacob Sartorius maintains a private personal life, living with his parents in Reston, Virginia, and actively engages with his fans on various social media platforms.
