    Chlöe Howl Facts

  • The diaeresis above the “o” in her name is allegedly a mistake made by her parents on her birth certificate.
  • Chlöe Howl spent her childhood years in Holyport, near Maidenhead, Berkshire.
  • She recorded and sold her own Christmas CD singing the song All I Want for Christmas Is You in order to help raise funds for her primary school.
  • On March 5, 2013, she released her debut EP, Rumour, and it included tracks such as I Wish I Could Tell You, No Strings, and Rumour.
  • Chlöe Howl’s song, No Strings, was released on August 23, 2013, via Sony Music Entertainment. It peaked at number #182 on the French Singles Chart and was also used as part of the Kick-Ass 2 movie soundtrack.
  • Along with Mø, Sam Smith, and Dan Croll, she was included in the “New Artists 2014” list curated by iTunes.
