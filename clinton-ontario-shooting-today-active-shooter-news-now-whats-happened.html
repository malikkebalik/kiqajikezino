    Incident Description

    Police Statement

    Huron OPP Const. Craig Soldan addressed the incident, stating that it appears to be a crime of opportunity. He refrained from commenting on the individual’s mental health or whether he was under the influence of alcohol or drugs at the time. The police are actively investigating the case and are urging anyone with information or potential video footage to come forward.

    It is important to note that this incident is an isolated case and does not reflect the overall safety of Clinton. Soldan emphasized that Clinton is generally a very safe place to live and work, and these incidents are exceptions rather than the rule. The local community wants people to feel safe and secure, and the police are taking all necessary measures to ensure the well-being of the residents.

    The alleged perpetrator is facing multiple charges, including sexual assault, assault, mischief, disorderly conduct, and failure to comply with probation and theft under $5,000. The police have detained two individuals in connection with the incident, and they remain in custody.
