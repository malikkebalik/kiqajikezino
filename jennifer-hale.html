    Planning to become a lawyer, she completed her undergraduate degree in Political Science at Louisiana State University, New Orleans. Her life took a different turn, however, when she appeared as a political analyst for Tiger TV, a student-run television program at LSU.

    She confesses that it was the moment she fell in love with broadcasting. Being a sports lover and a ‘competition gymnast’ led her to become captain of the cheerleading team, and she was also the homecoming queen and Miss LSU.

    Hale earned her Master’s degree in Journalism from Northwestern University in Chicago. She is also the recipient of the 2009 RIAS fellowship, which enables her to fly to Germany and research US-German ties.
