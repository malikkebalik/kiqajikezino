    If you are looking for extra comfort, big space, high ground clearance, and wider tires, perfect for tough roads or no roads. Then nothing can compare to SUV cars.  

    SUVs are the right fit for every occasion whether it is for your adventure journey with your friends or a long comfy trip with your family.

    But here’s the thing with high ground clearance and wider tyres, they exhaust the engine which means less mileage. But no worries, in this article you will find the 10 best SUV cars with good mileage that will save you a ton of money with your right SUV fit. 

    Best Mileage Suv in India 2024

    The table shows the 10 high mileage SUV cars and their price list:   

    Let’s dig a little deeper into these 10 best SUVs for great mileage cars in India: 

    1. Maruti Vitara Brezza

    The Maruti Vitara Brezza is one of the most popular SUVs in India and is the best mileage SUV car known for its excellent fuel efficiency. It is powered by a 1.5L Smart Hybrid petrol engine that delivers a mileage of up to 25.51 km/kg. The Brezza is one of the stylish SUVs that comes in dual-tone hot colors with its shark-looking antenna hard to miss from a distance. If you are looking for a style and sporty look with a good SUV then go for it.   

    Read More: Tata Nexon: What’s New and Exciting Futuristic SUV Features

    2. Tata Nexon

    The Tata Nexon is another maximum mileage SUV in India that is known for its stylish raised hood and dynamic stance design that looks more aggressive. It is powered by a 1.2L turbocharged petrol engine that delivers a mileage of up to 17.44 kmpl and the diesel variant gives a mileage of up to 24.08 kmpl. The Nexon is also a very safe SUV that comes with six airbags, having received a 5-star rating from Global NCAP.

    3. Hyundai Venue

    The Hyundai Venue is a stylish and feature-rich SUV with advanced new-age connectivity technology that is also known for its good mileage. It is powered by a Kappa 1.2 MPi petrol engine that delivers a mileage of up to 17.5 kmpl and for the diesel variant 23.4 kmpl. The new Knight Venue’s sleek, glamorous design with a powerful stance, alloy wheels, and dynamic lines makes it a more elite SUV.

    4. Mahindra XUV 300

    The Mahindra XUV 300 is a powerful and sporty SUV that comes with excellent mileage and fuel efficiency. It is powered by a 1.2L turbocharged petrol engine and 1.5L turbo diesel engine that delivers a mileage of up to 18.5 kmpl and 19.7 kmpl consecutively. The XUV 300 is also very well-equipped and available in a wide range of petrol, diesel and color variants. 

    Also Read: Honda Elevate vs Kia Seltos Compact SUV Comparison 

    5. Kia Sonet

    The Kia Sonet is a stylish and sophisticated SUV with great mileage. It is powered by a 1.2L petrol engine gives a mileage of 18.4 kmpl and an HTE Diesel engine which delivers a mileage of 24.1 kmpl. The Sonet is also very comfortable to drive and has a luxurious cabin.

    6. Tata Punch

    The Tata Punch is one of India’s compact SUVs that is known for its excellent fuel efficiency. It is powered by a 1.2L petrol engine that delivers a mileage of up to 20.09 kmpl. The  Punch is a Tata Motors product that is known for its robust cars. It is consider best mileage car in india under 10 lakh. The Punch is a very safe SUV, having received a 5-star rating from Global NCAP.

    7. Renault Kiger

    The Renault Kiger is a stylish and muscular SUV on a budget and one of the good mileage SUV cars in India. It is powered by a 1.0L petrol engine that delivers a mileage of up to 20.1 kmpl. Currently, Kiger is only available in the 4 Petrol variants. The Kiger is a perfect combo of the sports SUV and smart features. 

    Also Read: Citroen C3 Aircross, The Upcoming Rival in Mid-Range SUVs

    8. Nissan Magnite

    The Nissan Magnite is a combination of Japanese engineering and Indian manufacturing with a great mileage SUV in India. It is powered by an HRAO 1.0L petrol engine that delivers a mileage of up to 20 kmpl. The Magnite is also very spacious and comfortable for its size.

    9. Citroen C3

    The Citroen C3 is a quirky and stylish SUV that also has good fuel efficiency. It is powered by a 1.2L petrol engine that delivers a mileage of up to 19.3 kmpl. The C3 comes in 7 variants and 10 color options. 

    10. Hyundai Exter

    The Hyundai Exter is another name added to the list of most mileage SUVs in India. It is powered by a 1.2L Kappa petrol engine with 19.4 kmpl mileage and a 1.2L kappa Bi-fuel CNG engine that delivers a mileage of up to 27.1 km/kg. The Exter is also a very spacious and comfortable SUV for its size.

    So these are our top 10 picks for the SUV with the most mileage in India. These SUVs offer a combination of good fuel efficiency, spacious interiors, comfortable ride quality, and modern features.
