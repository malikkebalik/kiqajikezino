    Who Is Michael Dudikoff?

    Michael Dudikoff, son of Rita T. Girardin and Michael Joseph Stephen Senior, was born on October 8, 1954, in Redondo Beach, Los Angeles, California, USA. His birth name is Michael Joseph Stephen Dudikoff Junior. His father, Michael Joseph Stephen Senior, was born in New York, to John Dudikoff and Mary Bogdanoff, who were Russian immigrants, and his mother, Rita, was of French-Canadian descent.

    Michael graduated from West Torrance High School. Besides that, he got his degree in Child Psychology from Los Angeles Harbor College. Michael trained in karate, aikido, judo and in Brazilian Jujitsu. He trained in martial arts for his role in the first American Ninja movie. Prior to that, he had no training in martial arts.
